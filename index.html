About Uganza

Uganza is an aqualife farm that specializes in rearing and selling of the Clarias gariepinus widely known as Catfish in Nigeria and across Africa. Located at Iyana-Ilogbo, Ifo in Ogun State, our farm boasts of a highly hygenic environment and specialized marine biologists/farmers who tend to the fish to achieve maximum results and consumer satisfaction. We do hope you patronize us today.
